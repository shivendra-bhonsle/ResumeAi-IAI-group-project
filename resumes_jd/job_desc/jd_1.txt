Job Title: Backend Engineer (Python/FastAPI)
Company: Horizon DataWorks
Location: Austin, TX (Hybrid)
Experience Required: 3–5 years
Employment Type: Full-time

About the Role:
Horizon DataWorks is seeking a Backend Engineer who will help architect, build, and optimize scalable backend services supporting our next-generation data automation platform. You will work closely with data engineering, frontend, and infrastructure teams to create high-availability APIs and distributed systems used by enterprise customers. This role is ideal for an engineer who enjoys solving complex backend challenges, improving system performance, and contributing to a growing engineering culture grounded in reliability, quality, and automation.

You will be responsible for building robust microservices, driving improvements to our API layer, and influencing architectural decisions as we expand our core service capabilities.

Responsibilities:

Design, implement, and maintain scalable backend APIs using Python, FastAPI, and asynchronous architectures.

Build RESTful and event-driven services to support real-time data processing workflows.

Improve application performance by profiling bottlenecks and optimizing database and caching operations.

Collaborate with DevOps to deploy services across AWS using CI/CD pipelines.

Develop unit, integration, and load tests for all backend endpoints.

Implement secure authentication, authorization, and data-access patterns.

Work with product managers to translate business requirements into technical specifications.

Participate in code reviews and mentor junior engineers on best practices.

Monitor backend systems using metrics, tracing, and logging tools.

Contribute to technical design documents and long-term architectural roadmaps.

Required Skills:

Strong expertise in Python, FastAPI, asyncio, and RESTful service design.

Experience with SQL databases (PostgreSQL preferred).

Familiarity with Redis, Docker, and AWS services.

Strong understanding of microservices architecture and distributed systems.

Experience with observability tooling (Grafana, Prometheus, etc.).

Knowledge of API security principles and authentication standards (JWT/OAuth).

Excellent problem-solving skills and attention to detail.

Preferred Skills:

Experience with message queues (Kafka, RabbitMQ).

Knowledge of infrastructure-as-code frameworks such as Terraform.

Familiarity with event-driven architecture.

Experience building high-throughput, low-latency applications.

Education Requirements:
Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or related field.

$125,000–$155,000 base salary

Why Join Us:

High-impact role with ownership over core backend systems.

Work with a collaborative, senior engineering team.

Opportunities for career growth and architecture leadership.

Strong engineering culture with emphasis on automation and performance.

Competitive compensation, benefits, and hybrid flexibility.
