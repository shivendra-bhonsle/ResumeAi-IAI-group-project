Job Title: Marketing Data Analyst (SQL + Excel + Visualization)
Company: BrightSky Consumer Products
Location: Denver, CO (Hybrid)
Experience Required: 2–5 years
Employment Type: Full-time

About the Role:
BrightSky Consumer Products seeks a Marketing Data Analyst to help build a data-driven marketing strategy supporting brand campaigns, customer acquisition, and retention. You will analyze performance across paid media, CRM, and e-commerce funnels and work with marketing, product, and analytics teams to build actionable insights.

This is a high-impact role where your analysis will directly influence advertising spending, campaign optimization, and ROI tracking.

Responsibilities:

Analyze marketing funnel performance across paid search, social, email, and web channels.

Build SQL queries to extract and transform marketing datasets from multiple sources.

Develop and maintain performance dashboards with Excel, Power BI, or Tableau.

Provide weekly and monthly reporting for marketing leadership.

Conduct customer segmentation and cohort analysis to identify high-value audiences.

Support marketing attribution model improvements and experimentation.

Partner with media teams to guide budget allocation decisions.

Identify insights that optimize conversion rates and improve ROAS.

Establish data quality monitoring for marketing data feeds.

Present findings to non-technical stakeholders in clear, compelling formats.

Required Skills:

Strong SQL and Excel (power functions, lookups, modeling).

Experience with visualization tools (Power BI, Tableau, or Looker).

Knowledge of digital marketing metrics, funnel analysis, and attribution.

Experience working with large datasets.

Strong communication and data storytelling abilities.

Ability to work cross-functionally in fast-paced environments.

Preferred Skills:

Familiarity with Google Analytics, BigQuery, or ad-platform APIs.

Experience with Python for data cleaning or modeling.

Understanding of marketing automation systems (HubSpot, Marketo).

Experience with A/B testing and experimentation frameworks.

Education Requirements:
Bachelor’s degree in Marketing Analytics, Statistics, Business, or related field.

Compensation Range (optional):
$85,000–$105,000 base salary

Why Join Us:

Direct influence on marketing strategy and spending.

Collaborative environment with data-driven marketers.

Opportunity to lead analytical enhancements across campaigns.

Excellent benefits, hybrid schedule, and career growth pathways.
