Job Title: Product Analytics Data Scientist
Company: NovaStream Media
Location: New York, NY (Hybrid)
Experience Required: 3–6 years
Employment Type: Full-time

About the Role:
NovaStream Media is searching for a Product Analytics Data Scientist to influence product direction across our streaming and content-discovery platform. You will partner with product managers, designers, and engineers to uncover insights that power personalized recommendations, feature prioritization, and user-engagement improvements.

You will structure experiments, analyze behavioral data at scale, and communicate insights that shape the user experience for millions of customers.

Responsibilities:

Analyze user-behavior data to identify opportunities that improve engagement and retention.

Build dashboards and automated reporting that surface key product metrics.

Partner with product managers to define success metrics for feature launches.

Design, run, and interpret A/B tests to guide product decisions.

Conduct cohort, segmentation, funnel, and retention analyses.

Develop statistical models to predict user behavior and content preferences.

Collaborate with data engineering to improve data quality and pipelines.

Communicate complex analyses to stakeholders through visual and narrative storytelling.

Support long-term data strategy and analytics roadmap planning.

Mentor junior analysts and contribute to analytics best practices.

Required Skills:

Strong SQL and Python for data manipulation and modeling.

Knowledge of experimentation frameworks and causal inference.

Expertise in visualization tools (Tableau, Looker, Mode).

Familiarity with behavioral analytics, funnel analysis, and cohort modeling.

Strong statistical foundations and problem-solving skills.

Excellent communication and cross-functional collaboration abilities.

Preferred Skills:

Experience with recommendation systems or personalization.

Exposure to Spark or distributed computing frameworks.

Knowledge of product analytics tools (Amplitude, Mixpanel).

Experience in media or subscription-based products.

Education Requirements:
Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Data Science, Statistics, Computer Science, or similar field.

Compensation Range (optional):
$130,000–$165,000 base salary

Why Join Us:

Influence a product used by millions of people.

Work closely with world-class product and engineering teams.

Strong internal analytics culture and career advancement opportunities.

Hybrid flexibility with modern NYC workspace.
